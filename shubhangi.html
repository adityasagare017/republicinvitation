Join Us for a Spectacular Celebration!

Dear Friends,
As we commemorate the 73rd anniversary of India's Republic Day, we invite you to join us for a vibrant celebration at Abasaheb Garware College,Pune. This auspicious occasion is a testament to the values of democracy, unity, and freedom that form the foundation of our nation.

Highlights of the Event:

  1. Flag Hoisting Ceremony: Witness the unfurling of the tricolor flag with pride and reverence.
  2. Cultural Performances: Enjoy captivating dance and music performances showcasing the diverse cultural tapestry of India.
  3. Guest Speeches: Listen to inspiring speeches by esteemed guests reflecting on the significance of Republic Day and the journey of our nation.
  4. Refreshments: Indulge in delicious snacks and beverages served in the spirit of camaraderie.

Schedule:

  • 9:00 AM: Arrival and Registration
  • 9:30 AM: Flag Hoisting Ceremony
  • 10:00 AM: Cultural Performances
  • 11:00 AM: Guest Speeches
  • 11:30 AM: Refreshments and Networking

Attire:

Casual attire. Feel free to wear traditional Indian attire to celebrate our cultural heritage.
